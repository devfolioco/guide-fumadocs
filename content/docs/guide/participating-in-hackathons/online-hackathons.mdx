## Dashboard

The dashboard includes the following:

- **Timeline:** Displays key hackathon dates (start, end, and submission deadlines)
- **Announcements:** View important updates from organizers
- **Prizes:** Lists the available prizes and relevant details
- **Submitting Solo:** Allows an individual participant to upload and submit their project
- **Submitting in Team:** Lets a participant create/join a team (if not already done) and then submit the teamâ€™s project
